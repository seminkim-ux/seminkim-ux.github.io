Kumon APP

I designed the UI for the Kyowon Kumon official app renewal project.

I established the renewal concept and completed the design for all screens.

ROLE UI Design/ 100% PROGRESS Launching Updated App

Overview

Upgrading to an even better learning partner.

After the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, online education demand increased dramatically.
Kyowon Kumon was operating a service called Kumon Parents App, but needed to provide a more systematic educational service.
In particular, I designed the UX to emphasize Kumon's strength in learning content and to feel like an upgraded app.

Keyword

I identified the relationship between
target users and the service.

Over 50% of Kumon app users are parents who supervise learning.
We needed to consider both children/teenagers who manage their own learning and parents who guide them.
We also needed to consider adult learners as a new target audience.

Core Value

We established the values to be delivered to users
focused on two core functions: content and learning status guidance.
