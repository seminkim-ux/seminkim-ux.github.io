Overview

Optimal Mobile Sales Management.

This is a sales management app for Amore Pacific agency employees.
We needed to provide sales registration, business management, and inquiry tasks—previously limited to PC—in a mobile-friendly environment.
I designed a B2B app optimized for sales management with a focus on sales targets and streamlined screen composition for operational efficiency.

Concept

Target Concept Emphasizing Shared Goals.

The top of the main screen sequentially displays collection status, performance trends, and team member performance comparison graphs.
By continuously monitoring target-shaped graphs, users can focus on achieving sales goals.

Solution

Concise Start and Sequential Information Delivery

The main screen provides only the most essential information concisely rather than complex content.
